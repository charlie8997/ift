About Us

Who We Are

The Department of Information Technology at the Federal University of Technology, Minna, is committed to producing skilled, innovative, and industry-ready graduates equipped to thrive in today’s fast-evolving digital world. Established to address the growing demand for IT professionals in Nigeria and beyond, the department offers a robust academic foundation combined with hands-on experience in cutting-edge technologies.

Our Journey

The idea of the Department of Information Technology was conceived in 2016 at a School Board meeting of the School of Information and Communication Technology. After due processes, the Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) Information Technology Program was officially approved by the University Senate on 27th March, 2019.

The programme had a successful resource verification exercise by the National Universities Commission (NUC) in December 2020. Consequently, the B.Tech. Information Technology Programme commenced in the 2021/2022 academic session.

The programme is designed to build capacity and develop human capital to the highest level possible in the field of Information Technology. It aims to address computing application needs in different disciplines and organisations, thereby creating jobs and wealth.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To produce well-informed and competent professionals who are capable of imparting on Information Technologies nationally and globally.

Our Mission

To serve as an intellectual centre that will produce graduates with high competencies in Information Technologies that meet contemporary societal demands; as well as serve as a community resource for new ideas, partnerships, and collaborative efforts.
